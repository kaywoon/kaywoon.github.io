My Instant Noodle Recipes

Throughout my time of cooking at home, I have came up with various instant ramen recipes. Here are three of them that I would like to share! All of them are modified viral ramen recipes from the internet which I took inspiration from.

1. Kujirai Ramen

Ingredients for Kujirai Ramen

  • korean ramen (i used jin ramen but you can use shin)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 piece of sliced cheese

Steps:

  1. In a pan, add around 300ml of water and bring it to a boil
  2. Put in the noodle block, and flip it when the bottom side gets soft
  3. Once the noodles are 3/4 cooked, drain most of the water
  4. Add in the dried vegetable pack and half the seasoning pack, and mix it
  5. Add the slice cheese and egg, and cover until the cheese is melted
  6. Mix the whole dish until the everything is homogenous. Served!
